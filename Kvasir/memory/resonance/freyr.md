## The Norse Mythology

Freyr is a Vanir god — the fertility gods who came before the Aesir (Odin, Thor). He rules Álfheimr (realm of the light elves). He owns:

- **Gullinbursti** — the golden boar, shines in darkness, runs faster than any horse
- **Skíðblaðnir** — the magical ship, always has a tailwind, folds up small enough to pocket
- **Dainsleif** — a sword that fights on its own once drawn (he gave it up for love)

His great sacrifice: he gave up his magic sword to win Gerðr (a giantess he loved), leaving him weaponless at Ragnarök. He teaches that some things are worth trading power for.
